About Us

Welcome to Culm Valley Carpets

We are a family owned retail business based in Cullompton, Devon, with over 30 years experience in the flooring industry.

Serving the Culm Valley and beyond, we offer a huge selection of carpets, vinyls, LVT, wood and natural flooring at competetive and affordable prices.

Our experienced fitters have served the local area for many years and have an excellent reputation amoungst our regular customers.

For your convenience, we offer a free measuring and estimating service, along with a sample selection service from our mobile showroom.
